How do I report a civic issue?

To report a civic issue, visit our MereSahar website and click on the "user dashboard" button. Fill the name, Select the appropriate category (like potholes, street light, water overflow, dustbin overflow), provide a detailed description, add photos if possible, and specify the location by clicking on get location. Once submitted, you'll receive tracking details to monitor the status of your complaint.

How long does it take to resolve a reported issue?

Resolution time depends on the nature and complexity of the issue. Simple problems like garbage collection might be resolved within 24-48 hours, while infrastructure repairs could take several days or weeks. You can track the progress of your complaint in real-time through your dashboard, and you'll receive notifications at each stage of the resolution process.

Can I report issues anonymously?

Yes, but you should fill name as anonymous, & report issues anonymously. However, you can track your complaints, receive updates, and build a reputation as an active citizen. Anonymous reports are processed by the reporter.

Which municipal services can I access through MereSahar?

MereSahar provides access to various municipal services including potholes, street light, water overflow, dustbin overflow. These services are made available through our partnerships with municipal corporations.

Can I pay my property tax through MereSahar?

No, because it is only for reporting the issues not to pay property tax amount for municipal corporations.

Is there a mobile app for MereSahar?

No, MereSahar is available as a website for both iOS and Android devices. You can visit the website using link. The website offers all the features of the platform with additional benefits like tracking notifications and location-based services.

How secure is my data on MereSahar?

We are not taking your data security very seriously. MereSahar gives direct user dashboard to protect your personal information. We comply with data protection regulations and never share your data the name also with third parties without your consent. Regular security audits ensure our platform remains safe and secure.

What should I do if the website is not loading properly?

First, try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache and cookies, or try accessing the site in a different browser. If you're using the website, try updating to the latest version of chrome. If none of these solutions work, please contact our technical support team with details about the issue.
