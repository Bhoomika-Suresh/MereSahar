About Us

MereSahar is a civic engagement platform designed to help citizens report issues in their city and track their resolution in real-time. We aim to create smarter, more responsive, and cleaner cities by bridging the gap between citizens and municipal authorities.

Our mission is to empower communities with easy-to-use tools for reporting problems and ensuring accountability in resolving them.
